How renting works on Sevor

Clear steps from choosing an item → booking → payment → photos → return → reviews.

Explore List an item Create an account
1
Explore

Choose an item

Go to Explore, pick a listing, and open the item page to see photos, price, and location.

2
Item page

Tap “Book now”

From the item page, tap Book now to open the booking form.

3
Dates

Select the rental dates

Pick start & end date, then submit your booking request.

4
Notification

The owner gets a notification

The owner receives a “New booking request” notification and opens the request.

5
Deposit

Owner sets the deposit and accepts / rejects

The owner sets a deposit (security) then accepts or rejects the request.
Suggested rule: deposit should not exceed 20× the 1-day price.

6
Payment

Renter chooses payment method

Cash: rent only (no deposit), direct agreement — Sevor cannot guarantee safety.
Card: renter pays the rent first, then the deposit (recommended).

7
Pickup photos

Upload pickup photos (before)

The renter uploads photos before taking the item. This protects both sides.

8
Return flow

After photos, a Return button appears

After uploading the required photos, a button appears to continue to the return page.

9
Return photos

Upload return photos (during return)

This page is used only when returning the item. Return photos are proof of condition.

10
Review

Renter reviews the owner & the item

After return, the renter leaves a review about the owner and the item.

11
Review

Owner reviews the renter

The owner can review the renter (communication, care, on-time return, etc.).

12
Report

Report a problem (24h window)

If there is an issue, the renter/owner can submit a report with photos. The report button is available for 24 hours only. After that, it’s disabled and the deposit is released automatically.

Important:
• Card payment is safer (traceable + proof).
• Cash payments are direct agreements — Sevor cannot guarantee safety or handle theft cases.
• Deposit + before/after photos protect both sides.
