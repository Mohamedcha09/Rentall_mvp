Privacy Policy

At Sevor, we respect your privacy and strive to protect your personal data. By using our platform, you agree to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect
  • Basic data such as name, email address, and phone number upon registration.
  • Usage information such as visited pages and session duration.
  • Secure payment data through our partners like Stripe.
How We Use the Information
  • To facilitate booking and payment operations between owners and renters.
  • To enhance your experience and personalize the content provided to you.
  • To send notifications or updates related to your account.
Data Protection

We use security protocols (SSL/TLS) to protect your information during transmission and prevent unauthorized access to our databases.

Information Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties except in the following cases:

  • When using payment services like Stripe.
  • When legally required by official authorities.
User Rights

You can request to delete or modify your account data anytime from your profile page.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be published on this page along with the latest modification date.
