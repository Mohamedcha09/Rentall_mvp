Delete your Sevor account

This page explains how Sevor users can request deletion of their account and associated personal data.

How to request deletion

You can request deletion of your Sevor account in one of the following ways:

  • Send an email to no-reply@sevor.net from the same email address used for your Sevor account, and clearly state that you want to delete your account.
  • Or contact us from inside the app using the support / help section and ask for “Account deletion”.

We may ask you to confirm your identity to protect your account from unauthorized deletion.

What data will be deleted

When we delete your account, we permanently remove:

  • Your Sevor profile (name, photo, bio)
  • Your listings, saved items and favourites
  • Your messages and conversations (where legally possible)
  • Your reviews and ratings (or we anonymize them)

Data we may keep temporarily

Some information must be kept for a limited time for legal, security or fraud-prevention reasons, for example:

  • Completed bookings and payment records
  • Invoices and tax-related information

This data is kept only as long as necessary (up to 90 days) and is not used for marketing purposes. After this period, it is either deleted or anonymized.

Questions

If you have any questions about account deletion or data protection, you can contact us at no-reply@sevor.net.
